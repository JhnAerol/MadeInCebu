About MadeInUbec

Wear the Island. Own the Street.

Our Story

MadeInUbec is a curated clothing e-commerce platform dedicated exclusively to Cebu-inspired and homegrown streetwear brands. Born from a desire to elevate local craftsmanship to the global stage, we provide a unified storefront for the island’s finest apparel.

From the deep historic roots celebrated by SuroyWear to the high-end architectural cuts by UA Worldwide, to the classic island lifestyle depicted by HGHR and Strap — our catalog is a love letter to Philippine streetwear culture. Our mission is simple: to make premium, locally-made fashion accessible to everyone, everywhere.

About the Products

Every shirt and fashion piece on our platform represents the pinnacle of local manufacturing. We feature heavy 300GSM CVC fabrics that hold structural drapery, 100% hypoallergenic organic cotton for tropical comfort, and premium French terries with high-density ribbing.

The brands we carry don't just print shirts — they engineer them. Whether you're looking for an oversized, box-fit silhouette, a clean graphic tee with high-definition silk screening, or an everyday essential, you'll find clothes built to last through the wash, the weather, and the trends.
